Advanced Basketball Skills Training

Basketball IQ

Being able to handle the basketball is a big part of the game of basketball, and even if you are center you still need to work on your hands and develop your handles some. That is why it is important to work on your handles on a regular basis. Even if it is only for 20 minutes a day.

Ball Handling

Teaching the difference of using north/south and east/west to create space for better opportunity by using the triple set dribble for scoring and passing.

Shooting

Sessions are designed to teach players proper way to shoot the basketball. Each player will be taught how to shoot from various locations on the court with good balance, arch, back spin, and follow through.

Your level of preparation will always be revealed.
